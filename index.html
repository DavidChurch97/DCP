I am a developer/designer who enjoys creating interactive experiences in the browser either on mobile, desktop, laptop or tablets. I have an interest in frontend development with a small insight into running the backend with experience with creating & maintaining SQL databases.

I spent my time in education at Edinburgh college where I began to pique an interest in the user experience within web applications, from there I moved onto Napier University where I eventually graduated with a 2:1 classification in Web Design & Development.

This course allowed me to learn and experience working with users to plan, create & test my designs either that be prototyping or full-blown applications.

Featured Project

Little Boxes

Little Boxes is a VR puzzle game that has been developed to run in a browser via PC or mobile. Using the A-Frame framework I have managed to develop this experience for the use of a Google Cardboard.

This game was designed in progression with a study on how the users' experience can be improved within a VR environment. Looking at different UX methods I have managed to create an experience that a user can hopefully interact with without experiencing the usual symptoms of VR.
